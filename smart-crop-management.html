Our Technology

Powered by cutting-edge AI and data science

Remote Sensing

We utilize multi-spectral satellite imagery and drone surveys to monitor crop health, detect stress, and identify areas requiring attention. Our system processes NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) and other vegetation indices to provide accurate crop health assessment.

IoT Sensors

Our network of wireless soil moisture sensors, weather stations, and crop monitors collect real-time data from your fields. These solar-powered devices transmit information to our cloud platform, providing continuous monitoring with minimal maintenance.

Machine Learning

Our proprietary algorithms analyze millions of data points to identify patterns and make predictions. The system continuously learns from your farm's specific conditions, improving accuracy over time and adapting to your unique growing environment.

Mobile Integration

Access all insights and recommendations through our intuitive mobile app. Receive real-time alerts, view detailed reports, and manage your farm operations from anywhere. The app works offline and syncs when connectivity is available.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our Smart Crop Management system

How much does the system cost?

Our pricing is based on farm size and the specific modules you need. Basic packages start at ₹2,500 per acre per year, with discounts for larger farms. We also offer flexible payment options including seasonal billing to align with your cash flow. Contact us for a customized quote.

Do I need internet connectivity in my fields?

No, our system works in areas with limited connectivity. The IoT sensors use low-power wide-area network (LPWAN) technology to transmit data, and our mobile app can work offline, syncing when connectivity is available. For farms with no connectivity, we offer satellite communication options.

How long does it take to set up the system?

Initial setup typically takes 2-3 weeks, including field mapping, sensor installation, and system configuration. Our team handles the entire process, and you'll receive comprehensive training. The system becomes more accurate over time as it learns your specific farm conditions.

Can the system work for organic farming?

Absolutely! Our system is particularly valuable for organic farmers. It provides early pest and disease detection, allowing for timely application of organic treatments. The system also includes specific recommendations for organic inputs and can help with organic certification documentation.

What crops does the system support?

Our system supports a wide range of crops including cereals (rice, wheat, maize), pulses, oilseeds, fruits, vegetables, and commercial crops like cotton and sugarcane. We're continuously adding support for more crops based on farmer demand.
